Courses We Offer

At your choice, the following school programs are provided

Operational management

The course is integrative in nature and presents the overall coordinated management of the organization's operations. The course is structured as an ideology and technology for managing organically related business processes of an enterprise with different directions. Knowledge in the field of operational management focuses on improving the efficiency of the organization's business processes.

Marketing management

The course is aimed at mastering the principles and methods of marketing management of an enterprise, making strategic and tactical (operational) decisions within the framework of understanding marketing management.

Financial management

The trainees are introduced to the most common deviations from rational behavior in practice and their applications in finance, investment and strategic management. The course examines such well-known deviations as overconfidence, the “winner's curse” effect, the “hot hand” delusion, heuristics, overreaction and underreaction effects, noise trading, information cascades, the launch of investment imitation projects, the “sticking-in” effect, the disposition effect, etc. a lot others.
